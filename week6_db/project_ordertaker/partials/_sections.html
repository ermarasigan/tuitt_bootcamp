About


What is ördertaker?

A non-profit initiative to collect orders for food delivery from multiple users. The name was inspired by a song written by Parokya ni Edgar.

Hoy! wala na bang ma-o
Wala na bang ma-order, ma-order
Shet! Meron ngang menu
Wala namang ma-order, oh waiter
Sana naman may Siopao lang man o burger
Pa-order! Pa-order! Pa-order

How to order


It's easy and free of charge!

Log in or sign up
Filter the menu and choose
Add to cart and customize
Pay the ordertaker
